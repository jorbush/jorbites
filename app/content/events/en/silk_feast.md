# 🪡 The Silk Feast 🪡

The Silk Road comes alive on Jorbites! Explore the rich flavors of Uzbekistan, Georgia, and Armenia—cuisines where East meets West in the most delicious way.

## 🧁 Theme

Trade route cuisine that combines the best of three worlds: rice from Asia, pasta from Europe, and nuts from the East. These are exotic flavors that few people know, but with taste profiles that are wonderfully approachable.

Think cinnamon-spiced pilafs, roasted meats with pomegranate, yoghurt-based sauces, walnut pastes, aromatic herbs, and dishes that tell stories of ancient trade routes.

Dumplings, stuffed breads, hearty stews, fragrant rice dishes, and nut-laden desserts—all are welcome.
