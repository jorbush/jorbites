# 🥗 Salad Dressing Event 🥗

Discover the secret to elevating any salad on Jorbites!

A great dressing can transform simple ingredients into a gourmet experience. Whether it's a creamy ranch, a zesty vinaigrette, or a bold fusion sauce, we want to see how you dress up your greens.

### 🏆 Earn your exclusive badge

All Jorbiters who publish a dressing or vinaigrette recipe during the event window will receive the Salad Dressing badge.

**Requirement**: To qualify for the badge, you must select the **Sauces** category when posting your recipe.

### 🥗 Ideas to inspire you

- **Classic Vinaigrette**: The perfect balance of oil and vinegar.
- **Creamy Caesar**: A rich and savory favorite.
- **Honey Mustard**: Sweet, tangy, and versatile.
- **Asian Sesame**: Nutty and aromatic flavors.
- **Lemon Herb**: Fresh and bright for summer salads.
- **Balsamic Glaze**: Thick, sweet, and sophisticated.

### 🌿 Why the right dressing matters

Dressing is more than just a topping; it's the soul of the salad. It provides the essential acidity, fat, and seasoning that brings all the ingredients together. Experimenting with different textures and flavors allows you to create healthy yet incredibly satisfying meals. 🥗✨
