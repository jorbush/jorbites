# 🍔 Jorbites Burger Championship: Smash Time! 🍔

Get ready for the second Jorbites Burger Championship! This time, we are focusing on the art of the **Smash Burger**. 

## 🍳 A Live Cooking Showdown

Just like our first championship, the challenge is to **cook your burger live, on the spot, in front of all participants!**

- You can prepare your special sauces, pickles, or signature buns at home.
- The beef (or plant-based) patty must be smashed and cooked **live** on the griddle during the event.
- Final assembly must be completed on-site under the pressure of the clock!

Whether you participate solo or in a team, you'll have to show your precision, speed, and heat-control skills in real time.

## 👀 How It Works

1. **Prep at Home**: Prepare any sauces, customized toppings, or bun recipes beforehand.
2. **Bring Your Ingredients**: Bring everything to the cooking station (except the cooked patty).
3. **Smash and Cook Live**: Press that patty paper-thin on a scorching hot griddle to achieve those legendary crispy, lacy caramelized edges.
4. **Publish Your Recipe**: Post your smash burger recipe on Jorbites right after your cooking session.

## 🏅 Awards & Badges

We've updated the reward system for this championship to celebrate everyone's effort:

* **For All Participants**: Every single chef who participates in the live cooking session and uploads their recipe will receive the exclusive **"Smash Master"** digital badge!
* **For The Champion**: The overall winner, determined by our panel of secret tasters, will receive the "Smash Champion" title and a mysterious, exclusive physical award that will only be revealed on the day of the championship!

**Ready to bring the heat and press to impress? See you on August 8th!**
