# 🍹 Summer Sips: Jorbites Cocktail Contest 🍹

Welcome to the ultimate summer mixology showdown on Jorbites! As the summer heat reaches its peak, it’s time to cool down with your most creative, refreshing, and beautiful drink creations.

We are calling all home bartenders and mixologists to share their best liquid recipes—whether they are alcohol-infused cocktails or vibrant, alcohol-free mocktails. Show us your unique flavor combinations, stunning garnishes, and colorful presentations!

## 🧪 Contest Guidelines

1. **Craft Your Drink**: Create a refreshing cocktail or mocktail perfect for hot summer days.
2. **Present it with Style**: Pay attention to the glassware, ice (crushed, clear blocks, or spheres), and garnishes (fresh herbs, citrus wheels, edible flowers).
3. **Publish Your Recipe**: Post the recipe on Jorbites on July 13th, detailing the exact measurements and preparation steps.

## 🏅 Awards & Badges

Celebrate summer and mixology with these rewards:

* **For All Participants**: Everyone who submits a summer drink recipe during the contest will receive the exclusive digital **"Summer Mixologist"** badge.
* **For The Champion**: The creator of the most innovative and beautifully presented drink will receive the "Mixology Legend" title and a mysterious physical award to showcase in their kitchen!

**Dust off your shakers, grab some fresh mint, and let's stir up some summer magic! See you on July 13th!**
