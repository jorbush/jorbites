# 🌮 Mexican Fiesta 🌮

Bring the heat and the flavor to Jorbites this May!

Mexican cuisine is one of the most beloved and diverse in the world. From the smoky spice of chipotle to the fresh zing of lime and cilantro, we want to see your best Mexican-inspired creations. Whether it's street-style tacos, a rich mole, or a refreshing pico de gallo, it's time to celebrate the fiesta!

### 🏆 Earn your exclusive badge

All Jorbiters who publish a Mexican-inspired recipe during the event window will receive the Mexican Fiesta badge.

**Requirement**: To qualify for the badge, you must select the **Mexican** category when posting your recipe.

### 🌮 Ideas to inspire you

- **Authentic Tacos**: Carnitas, al pastor, or mushroom asada.
- **Homemade Salsas**: From mild verde to fiery habanero.
- **Guacamole Variations**: Traditional or with a unique twist.
- **Enchiladas & Tamales**: Classic comfort foods.
- **Mexican Street Corn (Elote)**: The perfect side dish.
- **Churros with Chocolate**: A sweet finish to your feast.

### 🌶️ Why Mexican cuisine?

Mexican food is a celebration of community, tradition, and bold ingredients. It's about finding the perfect balance between spicy, sour, salty, and sweet. Exploring these flavors not only broadens your culinary horizons but also brings a burst of joy to your kitchen. ¡Buen provecho! 🌮✨
