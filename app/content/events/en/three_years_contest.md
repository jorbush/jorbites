# 🥂 Three Years of Jorbites 🥂

Three years ago, Jorbites began as a small project to bring food lovers together. Today, it’s a thriving community where thousands of stories are told through flavors, textures, and shared meals.

To celebrate this huge milestone, we’re hosting our 3rd-anniversary contest!

## 🎂 Theme: Express Party Bites

For our 3rd anniversary, we want to see your **Express Party Bites**. We’re looking for delicious, festive recipes that can be prepared in **less than an hour**. Simple, fast, and perfect for a celebration!

Think quick tapas, easy-to-assemble appetizers, or any celebratory dish that won't keep you in the kitchen all day.

## 📆 Date

Saturday, May 30, 2026 – Celebration time

## 🏆 Prize
- An exclusive Jorbites 3rd Anniversary commemorative gift.
- The "Three Years Legend" exclusive medal for the winner.

## 📌 Rules
- ✅ **New Recipes Only**: This contest is for **brand-new** recipes published for the first time on Jorbites.
- ✅ **Draft Mode**: Your recipe must be in draft mode (cloud icon) before the event begins.
- ✅ **Live Publishing**: The recipe must be published during the contest window.
- ❌ **Timing**: Publishing before or after the event window will result in a 2-point deduction.
- 👫 **Team Up**: You can participate as a pair using the co-authoring feature.
- 🎭 **Bonus Points**: Join the celebration in style! Coming dressed in festive attire (party hats, sequins, or a glass in hand) can earn you +1 or +2 bonus points.

Thank you for being the heart of Jorbites. Three years of cooking, learning, and laughing together. Let’s make this the most delicious anniversary yet!
