# 🍫 Chocolate Lovers! 🍫

Attention all chocoholics! It’s time to celebrate the world’s most beloved ingredient on Jorbites.

From the deepest dark chocolate to smooth milk and creamy white, we want to see your most indulgent creations. Whether it’s a decadent cake, artisanal truffles, or a surprising savory dish with a hint of cocoa, it’s time to celebrate the fiesta!

### 🏆 Earn your exclusive badge

All Jorbiters who publish a chocolate-themed recipe during the event window will receive the Chocolate Lovers badge.

**Requirement**: To qualify for the badge, your recipe must feature chocolate as a key ingredient and be published during the event window.

### 🍫 Ideas to inspire you

- **Decadent Cakes**: Lava cakes, flourless tortes, or classic fudgy brownies.
- **Handmade Treats**: Truffles, chocolate-covered fruits, or bark.
- **Breakfast Delights**: Chocolate chip pancakes or rich cocoa smoothies.
- **Creative Savory**: Mole-inspired sauces or chocolate-rubbed meats.
- **Healthy Indulgence**: Raw cacao energy balls or avocado chocolate mousse.

### 🌶️ Why we love chocolate

Chocolate is more than just a sweet treat; it’s a complex ingredient with hundreds of flavor profiles. From its ancient roots as a ceremonial drink to its modern status as a gourmet luxury, chocolate brings joy and comfort to kitchens everywhere. Experiment with different cocoa percentages and pairings to find your perfect match. 🍫✨
