# 📱 TikTok Recipes 🍳

Explore the most viral and trending culinary creations from TikTok on Jorbites!

Recreate your favorite TikTok food trends—from the famous baked feta pasta and pesto eggs to the latest viral treats. Share your version of these trending recipes and remember to post the original video link in a comment once published!

### 🏆 Earn your exclusive badge

All Jorbiters who publish a TikTok-inspired recipe during the event window will receive the TikTok Recipes badge.

### 💡 Rules & Requirements

- **Post a viral recipe**: Share a recipe that is trending or has gone viral on TikTok.
- **Link the original**: Once you publish your recipe, leave a comment on it with the link to the original TikTok video.

### 🍽️ Ideas to inspire you

- **Baked Feta Pasta**: The legendary cherry tomato and feta oven-bake.
- **Pesto Eggs**: Eggs fried directly in flavorful basil pesto.
- **Tortilla Wrap Hack**: The clever four-quadrant folding technique.
- **Green Goddess Salad**: Finely diced cabbage and spinach salad with a creamy pesto-like dressing.
- **Dalgona Coffee**: Whipped instant coffee over cold milk.
- **Crispy Accordion Potatoes**: Intricately cut potatoes roasted to crispy perfection.

### 🚀 What makes it special

TikTok has revolutionized the way we discover food, turning simple ingredients into global culinary sensations overnight. It's all about creativity, quick hacks, and sharing the joy of cooking with millions. Grab your phone, get cooking, and show us your take on these viral hits! 📱
