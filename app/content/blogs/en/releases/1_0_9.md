# 🚀 Jorbites v1.0.9 is here!

This version brings a new thematic event, fresh travel and food stories in our blog, and important security updates to keep the platform running smoothly.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Salad Dressing Event**: A new event has been added to explore the best ways to dress your greens.
- ✨ **New Blog Posts**: Read about our latest culinary adventures in Pamplona & Logroño and our visit to Frankfurt for some authentic pretzels.
- ✨ **Dependency Updates**: We've updated `follow-redirects`, `i18next-http-backend`, and `postcss` to their latest versions to ensure better security and performance.
- ✨ **v1.0.8 Release Blog**: The official blog post for the v1.0.8 release is now available.
