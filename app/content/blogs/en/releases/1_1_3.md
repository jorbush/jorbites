# 🚀 Jorbites v1.1.3 is here!

In this update, we've added a share action for public lists, making it easier to share your favorite recipe collections. We've also improved date localization and updated internal dependencies.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Share Public Lists**: Added a share button to the ListView header for public lists, allowing you to easily share your lists with others.
- ✨ **Improved Localization**: Dates in ListsView and other client components are now properly translated according to the selected language.
- ✨ **v1.1.2 Release Blog**: Added the official blog posts for the v1.1.2 release.
- ✨ **Dependency Update**: Updated `fast-uri` to the latest version for improved security and performance.
