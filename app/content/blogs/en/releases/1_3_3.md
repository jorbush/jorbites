# 🚀 Jorbites v1.3.3 is here!

We're excited to bring you Jorbites v1.3.3! This update includes updates to our 3 Years Contest prizes, the official v1.3.2 release blogs, and minor UI and metadata adjustments.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Events**: Updated the prizes for the 3 Years Contest across English, Spanish, and Catalan.
- ✨ **Release Blogs**: Added the official blog posts for the v1.3.2 release and updated the v1.3.1 release date to May 22nd.
- ✨ **UI/UX**: Removed the "New" indicator from the "My Lists" menu item for a cleaner navigation experience.
