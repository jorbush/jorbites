# 🚀 Jorbites v1.1.0 is here!

This version brings exciting new events like the Mexican Fiesta and the Three Years Contest, along with a highly requested feature: Lists. We've also included the official blog posts for the previous v1.0.9 release.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Mexican Fiesta Event**: Celebrate with our new Mexican-themed event and discover delicious recipes.
- ✨ **Three Years Contest**: Join our three-year anniversary celebration with this special contest.
- ✨ **Lists Feature**: You can now organize your favorite recipes and content into custom lists.
- ✨ **v1.0.9 Release Blog**: The official blog posts for the v1.0.9 release are now available.
