# 🚀 Jorbites v2.7.0 is here!

We're excited to announce the release of Jorbites v2.7.0! This update brings highly requested features like community lists and comment likes, along with a brand new courses page and significant technical improvements under the hood.

## ✨ What's new

- 👥 **Community Lists**: Discover and share recipe lists with the community for better inspiration.
- 🎓 **Courses Page**: A new dedicated space to explore culinary courses and improve your skills.
- 💬 **Comment Likes**: Show your appreciation for helpful tips and feedback by liking comments.
- 🍱 **New Recipe Fields**: We've added more details to recipe displays to give you a more complete cooking guide.

## 🛠️ Technical Improvements

- 🤖 **Tagatoni Microservice**: Integrated the Tagatoni microservice into our ecosystem to enhance recipe enrichment.
- ⚡ **React Compiler Optimizations**: Updated our routing logic to destructure `push` from `useRouter`, ensuring compatibility with the React Compiler.
- ♿ **Accessibility & UI Fixes**: Improved accessibility by fixing label associations in course components and refined typography by adjusting heading weights.
- 🧹 **State Management**: Refined the `ListsClient` component to resolve state mirroring issues, leading to more predictable rendering.
