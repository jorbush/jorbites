# 🚀 Jorbites v2.8.1 is here!

We are excited to announce Jorbites v2.8.1, featuring a brand-new community event, enhanced search usability, and important system fixes.

## ✨ What's new

- 📱 **TikTok Recipes Event**: Join our latest community challenge! Recreate and share your favorite viral TikTok recipes, link the original videos, and earn an exclusive badge.
- 🔍 **Polished Search**: Enjoy a cleaner search bar experience and a smarter search indicator that no longer triggers unnecessarily when changing the recipe sort order.

## 🛠️ Technical Improvements

- 🧪 **Hydration Fix**: Resolved a Next.js hydration mismatch on the recipe list sort dropdown to ensure a seamless load experience.
- 📦 **Dependency Updates**: Upgraded `axios` from 1.17.0 to 1.18.0 for improved security and performance.
