# 🚀 Jorbites v1.3.2 is here!

We're excited to bring you Jorbites v1.3.2! This update focuses on enhancing our liquid glass design, improving translations, and increasing the stability of our testing suite.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Design**: Added more liquid glass styling and fixed the top bar for a more polished visual experience.
- ✨ **Translations**: Fixed Catalan translation for the "Most Liked" section to ensure accuracy.
- ✨ **Testing**: Improved test stability by fixing flaky Vitest tests in RootLayout.
- ✨ **v1.3.1 Release Blog**: Added the official blog posts for the v1.3.1 release.
