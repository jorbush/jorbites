# 🚀 Jorbites v1.3.2 is here!

We're excited to bring you Jorbites v1.3.2! This update focuses on enhancing the UI with more liquid glass styling, fixing navigation issues, and improving development stability.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **UI/UX**: Added more liquid glass styling across the app and fixed the top bar layout.
- ✨ **Translations**: Fixed the Catalan translation for "Most Liked" (Més agradades).
- ✨ **Testing**: Improved Vitest stability by fixing flaky tests in the RootLayout.
- ✨ **v1.3.1 Release Blog**: Added the official blog posts for the v1.3.1 release.
