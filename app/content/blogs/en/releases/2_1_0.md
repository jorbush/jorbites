# 🚀 Jorbites v2.1.0 is here!

We're excited to share the release of Jorbites v2.1.0! This update brings a highly requested feature: review ratings, allowing you to better share your experiences. We've also refined our event management and updated current seasonal events.

## ✨ What's new
- ⭐ **Review Ratings**: You can now rate your experiences when leaving reviews.
- 📅 **Events**: Updated the Summer Sips Contest date to July 11th.
- 📝 **Events**: Shortened titles for Taste of Japan and Fit & Fresh for better readability.
- 🛠️ **Improvements**: Fixed errors in new event handling.
- 📖 **Release Blogs**: Added the official blog post for the v2.0.1 release.
