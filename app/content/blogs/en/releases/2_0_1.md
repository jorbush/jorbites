# 🚀 Jorbites v2.0.1 is here!

We're happy to announce the release of Jorbites v2.0.1! This version focuses on bringing more exciting events to the community, including a new burger championship and several seasonal food and drink festivals.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Events**: Added the Second Burger Championship (Smash Time) event.
- ✨ **Events**: Added the Fit & Fresh event.
- ✨ **Events**: Added the Taste of Japan event.
- ✨ **Events**: Added the Summer Sips Contest event.
- ✨ **Release Blogs**: Added the official blog posts for the v2.0.0 release.
