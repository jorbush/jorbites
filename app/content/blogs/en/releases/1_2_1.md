# 🚀 Jorbites v1.2.1 is here!

We're excited to bring you Jorbites v1.2.1! This update focuses on strengthening our infrastructure, optimizing performance with new caching layers, and continuing to provide fresh content for our community events.

## ✨ What's new
- ✨ **Infrastructure Isolation**: Isolated Kafka and Redis services to improve system reliability and scalability.
- ✨ **Performance & Stability**: Fixed Kafka timeout issues and implemented Redis caching for recipe pages to ensure a faster and smoother experience.
- ✨ **API Improvements**: Enhanced the Weekly Challenge API endpoints for better performance and maintainability.
- ✨ **Event Updates**: Added new recipe ideas for the Three Years Contest event to inspire our community.
- ✨ **v1.2.0 Release Blog**: Added the official blog posts for the v1.2.0 release.
