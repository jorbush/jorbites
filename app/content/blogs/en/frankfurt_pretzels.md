## Pretzel tier list:

- **Tier 3 – Carbonara**: It tasted like a supermarket carbonara pizza but with pretzel dough (better in my opinion).
- **Tier 2 – Brie Cheese**: It was tasty but in my opinion it lacked serrano ham and had too much butter.
- **Tier 1 – The Classic**: It has salt and something like a non-spicy pepper. Very flavorful despite being the classic, and reminds me of the snack with small pretzels you can buy in Barcelona but much better.
